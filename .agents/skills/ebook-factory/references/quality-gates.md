# Quality Gates

## Gate 1: Brief

- Audience is explicit.
- Promise is specific but not exaggerated.
- Scope is narrow enough for the requested length.
- Sensitive domains are flagged.

## Gate 2: Research

- Permanent research corpora were searched with `--source research`.
- Topic corpus exists or the reason for skipping is stated.
- Claims are tied to retrieved context where possible.
- Recent or high-stakes claims are not invented from memory.

## Gate 3: Outline

- Chapters progress from foundations to application.
- Each chapter has a job.
- Exercises match the promise.
- Appendices add utility, not filler.

## Gate 4: Manuscript

- No unsupported guarantees.
- No repetitive chapter openings.
- Advice is actionable.
- Examples are concrete.
- Caveats are clear but not fear-heavy.

## Gate 5: Money/Business Topics

- No guaranteed income.
- No fake scarcity or manipulative promises.
- Scams and risks are named.
- Legal/tax/platform dependency caveats are included.
- The ebook separates skills, business models, tools, and execution plans.

## Gate 6: PDF

- `pdfinfo` page count reported.
- `pdftoppm` previews inspected.
- Cover bleed is clean.
- Page backgrounds fill margins.
- No blank overflow pages after special pages.
- TOC limitation is disclosed if Chrome cannot provide page numbers.
