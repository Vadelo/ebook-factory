# Ebook Factory Business Ethics

## Money Topics

Ebooks about income, business, marketing, investing, online work, AI automation, or side hustles must avoid guaranteed outcomes. They should teach models, skills, risks, and decision-making.

## Required Boundaries

Include boundaries when relevant:

- no income guarantee;
- results depend on skills, market, effort, timing, and execution;
- legal and tax duties vary by location;
- platforms can change rules;
- scams and predatory offers exist.

## Good Structure For "Make Money Online"

1. Reality check: what is and is not realistic.
2. Business models: freelancing, services, content, products, affiliate, ecommerce, education, software.
3. Skill map: sales, writing, design, automation, analytics, support.
4. Selection framework: choose based on time, capital, risk, skill, market.
5. Execution plan: validate, offer, traffic, conversion, delivery, retention.
6. Risks: scams, platform dependency, taxes, burnout, false promises.
7. 30-day action plan.

## Ethical Copy

Do not use:

- "renda garantida";
- "formula secreta";
- "sem esforco";
- "qualquer pessoa fica rica";
- fake urgency;
- fake testimonials.

Use:

- "caminhos possiveis";
- "habilidades monetizaveis";
- "plano de validacao";
- "expectativas realistas";
- "riscos e trade-offs".
